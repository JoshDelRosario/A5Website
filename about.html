Who We Are

Mostly Mad Music is a charity which runs events, delivers music programs and creates mixed platform multimedia content. We use music as an icebreaker and to start difficult conversations about mental health. Our live events create relaxed and vibrant settings where people from all backgrounds can come together and share positive and moving experiences.

Our multimedia content targets hard to reach demographics, building engagement and creating empathy in audiences.

We believe music transcends difference and forges connections; and as a growing community we aim to use tailored strategies to explore the relationship between music, mental illness and mental health.
