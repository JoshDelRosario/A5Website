Our Programs

We believe in the importance of giving everybody the chance to hear beautiful music. So in our first year, 2018, Mostly Mad Music’s flagship program – In Harmony – ran for the first time. We took classical music from the concert hall to people with a lived experience of mental illness and homelessness in the lead up to Mental Health Month. And we made a mini-documentary about our experience.

In 2019, we took the same idea and made it bigger and better – this time taking music to the streets and the people of Sydney. Our musicians performed pop up concerts in Martin Place, Hyde Park, Circular Quay and Darling Harbour.
